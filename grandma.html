Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generation
and never fails delight

Imgredient

  1. 1/2 cup sugar
  2. 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  3. 6 medium-sized apple, peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. preheat oven to 375^F (190^c).
  2. In a bowl, mix the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture.
